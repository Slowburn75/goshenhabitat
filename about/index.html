About Us

Building the future of Nigeria with integrity, innovation, and excellence.

Who We Are

Goshen Habitat Concept Limited is a premier architectural and construction firm based in Nigeria. Registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), we deliver high-quality residential, commercial, and institutional projects.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to transform the Nigerian landscape through sustainable and innovative construction practices.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results using modern construction technologies and best practices.

Our services include Architectural and Structural Design, Construction, Project Management, Facility Management, Consultancy Services, and Environmental Conservation.

We build long-term relationships founded on trust, transparency, and consistent performance.

Our Mission

To deliver superior architectural and construction solutions that exceed client expectations through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and timely delivery, while contributing to the sustainable development of Nigeria's infrastructure.

Our Vision

To be the leading architectural and construction firm in Nigeria, recognized for our integrity, excellence, and commitment to creating spaces that inspire and endure.

Core Values

  • • Quality: Excellence in every detail.
  • • Innovation: Modern solutions for modern needs.
