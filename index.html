About Us

Who We Are, with an individual approach

Goshen Habitat Concept Limited is a duly registered architectural and construction company under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, in accordance with the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA), 1990.

We are an established firm with a strong commitment to excellence, delivering high-quality architectural, construction, and project delivery solutions across Nigeria. At Goshen Habitat Concept Limited, quality, integrity, and client satisfaction remain at the core of everything we do. We never compromise on standards, workmanship, or service delivery.
